How to Use YouTube's Incognito Mode

Android: Sometimes you want to watch a video on YouTube and not have it show up in your history. For those moments when you need to go undercover, YouTube recently added an Incognito mode that makes it easier to watch videos without leaving a trace.The feature is currently only available for Android, and there’s no word if, or when, it’ll make it to the iOS version of the app.Also, know that the feature might prevent anything you watch from showing up in your history in the app, but your school or employer will still be able to see what you’re up to, depending on what methods they use to police their networks. To turn on Incognito mode, launch the YouTube app and tap on your avatar. From there, select “Turn on Incognito” from the menu that appears..
